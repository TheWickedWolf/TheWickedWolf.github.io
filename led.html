About

Medium: Photography

Genre: Mimicry

Description

This is a set of photos from my photography class midterm. We were tasked to copy an artists work and try and replicate it the best we could through our photography. I copyed someone who did a set of photos of an old LED T.v. close up. I went and took photos during the 2018 olympics on my old T.v. i took over 300 photos and these were the best. My favorite is the Coca-cola logo because I didn't edit it in photoshop, that was just how the light was captured in my phtograph.
