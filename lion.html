Classic Rock Experimentation

About

Medium: Ink, Markers, and Paint on Paper

Genre: Abstract Drawing & Experimentation

Size: 4ft x 4t

Description

This painting is an experimental piece created during a drawing class at the University of Iowa. We were tasked with listening to a certain type of music and then drawing what it sounded like. I chose to draw about classic rock because it's what helps me to focus on art. This piece was meant to be abstract but I really hate that type of art, so I went with what my gut told me. The imagery from the songs I got was a lot of sharp and fluid sounds, while also being loud and strong. I painted the lions to represent this and the raven for the quieter moments from the music.
