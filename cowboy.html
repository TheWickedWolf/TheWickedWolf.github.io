The Cowboy

About

Medium: Ink on Paper

Genre: Drawing

Size: 2ft x 2ft

Description

During drawing class at the University of Iowa, we were tasked to copy a painting from somebody only using ink to flesh out the greyscale of that particular painting. I used a painting by Frank Tenney Johnson, a famous Iowan western painter from the late 1800's to early 1900's. I really like his style and his subject matter so I thought I'd try and copy one of his works. I sketched it out first and then worked in the harsh black shadows first keep the scale right. This peice took me around 4 hours all together and It was my favorite project from drawing class.
