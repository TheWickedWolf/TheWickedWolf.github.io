About

Hello!

My name is Hannah Turner. I am a junior at Grandview University studying Game Design and Interactive Analytics.

My experience as a fine artist began when I could hold a crayon. I like to create realistic images of scenery and animals. My work has been accepted and honored in several Iowa State Fair art shows. I like any kind of medium, but I mostly work in pastels and paint.

I attended the University of Iowa for two years and studied Art. During that time I developed a love for graphic design and digital illustration. I transfered to Grand View to study Game Design. I enjoy expanding my expertise as a digital designer for web and game development.

Nature inspires me to create and design. I like to challenge myself by trying new techniques and mixing mediums to create unique designs.
