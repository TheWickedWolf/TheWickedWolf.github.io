About

Hello!

My name is Hannah Turner. I am a junior in college currently studying Game Design and Interactive Anyalitics at Grand View University.

My experience as a fine artist goes back since I could draw. I like to create realistic recreations of scenery and animals. I have been in several Iowa State Fair art shows. Otherwise I like any kind of medium but I mostly work in pastels and paint.

I went to the University of Iowa for my freshman and sophomore years studying art generally until I found a major that I liked at Grand View. In those first two years I developed a love for graphic design and digital illustration. Now I am working on my experience as a digital designer for web and game development.

What inspires me to create and design are anything that fits my fancy. I like to challenge myself by trying new techniques and mix mediums to create whole new ideas.
